Fintel - Financial Intelligence CLI

I built this as my personal stock research dashboard. It aggregates data from multiple financial APIs to calculate market sentiment, then runs a scoring algorithm to surface stock picks across short, medium, and long term timeframes with conviction ratings. The headwinds section tracks macro indicators like the Sahm Rule and dollar index, mapping their impact to specific sectors. Built in Python with async data pipelines so everything stays snappy even when pulling from a dozen sources at once.
