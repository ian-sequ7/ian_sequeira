Stock Scoring Engine

A 6-factor algorithm grounded in academic research: Momentum (25%), Quality (25%), Valuation (20%), Growth (15%), Analyst consensus (10%), and Smart Money flows (5%). Each stock gets scored and ranked to surface the most compelling opportunities.

Smart Money Tracking

Tracks hedge fund 13F filings, insider Form 4 transactions, and Congressional trades. When insiders are buying or activist funds are taking positions, that signal gets factored into the model.

Macro Risk Analysis

Monitors 22+ economic indicators across 8 categories: credit spreads, housing, recession probability (Sahm Rule), financial stress, inflation, labor market, valuations, and currency dynamics. Each indicator maps to specific sector impacts.

Additional Features

Daily market briefings with economic calendar and historical reaction patterns. Portfolio analytics with paper trading, P&L tracking, Sharpe ratio, and tax-loss harvesting. TradingView integration for technical analysis.

Tech Stack

Python 3.11+ with async httpx, SQLite for persistence. Frontend built with Astro 5, React 19, and TailwindCSS 4.
