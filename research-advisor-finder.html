Research Advisor Finder

About This Project

Finding a PhD advisor is tough, so I made this tool to help. It uses OpenAI embeddings to convert your research interests into vectors, then runs similarity search against a PostgreSQL database with pgvector to find professors whose published work aligns with what you want to study. The backend is FastAPI serving a Next.js frontend, and I integrated the Claude API to generate personalized explanations of why each professor might be a good match based on their actual papers.
